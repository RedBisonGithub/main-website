In 2019, Red Bison disrupted the network edge launching a security first, next generation building connectivity platform and business model for the commercial real estate (CRE) and multifamily (MFU) markets. Its proven connectivity solution with dual entry fiber paths integrates connections for all operational, property (PropTech), and tenant-based solutions providing visibility to performance data unavailable in current market solutions. Solving these technology challenges, Red Bison enables significant cost savings, greater efficiencies, and new revenue streams for building owners and operators.
